JavaScript Runs Everything (Almost)
JavaScript is estimated to be running on over 3 billion devices worldwide – smartphones,
 desktops, ATMs, medical devices, Kindles, and more. On top of that, JavaScript plays a 
 major part in the Internet of Things (IoT) and Cloud development.
